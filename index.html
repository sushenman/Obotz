Funtronix

Explore electronics through simple circuits, sensors, motors, and safe hands-on activities that make everyday technology easy to understand.

Robotics

Build confidence with mechanical parts, wheels, gears, and guided robot assembly while learning how motion works.

Solarix

Connect coding logic with real robot movement using loops, conditions, sequencing, and interactive challenges.
